How to Prepare Well for the ESPRIT Forum?

The ESPRIT Forum is an opportunity to meet with companies. It's now up to you, the student, to make the most of this event for your projects and future.

CV
Resume

Bring your resume after having carefully prepared it (no errors, appropriate formatting…)

Interviews
Interviews

Prepare for your interviews:

  • Research the company
  • Define what you expect from this company (an internship, a first job, what position...)
  • Dress appropriately for the interview (a suit isn't mandatory but too casual attire isn’t advisable)
LinkedIn
LinkedIn

Registering on LinkedIn would be a good way to easily maintain contact with companies and build your network.
